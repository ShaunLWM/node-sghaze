The hotspots are derived from satellite data and may be affected by cloud cover and the satellite coverage

Dry weather prevailed over most parts of northern ASEAN and is expected to persist for the next few days. In Cambodia, the dry weather contributed to persistent hotspot clusters from which moderate smoke haze was observed to emanate. Elsewhere, isolated hotspots were detected in Myanmar and Thailand. In the southern ASEAN region, there were isolated shower activities, which are forecast to continue. The winds in the northern ASEAN region are forecast to continue to blow from the northeast or east, while winds in the southern ASEAN region are expected to blow from the northeast or north.
